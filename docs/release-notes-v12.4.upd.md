This update to the Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL provides a technical preview of [pg_stat_monitor](https://github.com/percona/pg_stat_monitor) - the statistics collection tool for PostgreSQL. `pg_stat_monitor` is included in the set of extensions supplied within Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL.

We welcome your feedback on your experience with `pg_stat_monitor` in the [public JIRA project](https://jira.percona.com/projects/DISTPG).
