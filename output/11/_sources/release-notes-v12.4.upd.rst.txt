================================================================================
*Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL* 12.4 Update
================================================================================

:Date: September 22, 2020
:Installation: `Installing Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL <https://www.percona.com/doc/postgresql/12/installing.html>`_


This update to the |pdp| provides a technical preview of `pg_stat_monitor <https://github.com/percona/pg_stat_monitor>`_ - the statistics collection tool for |postgresql|. ``pg_stat_monitor`` is included in the set of extensions supplied within |pdp|.

We welcome your feedback on your experience with ``pg_stat_monitor`` in the `public JIRA project <https://jira.percona.com/projects/DISTPG>`_.
